Yesterday, I went with my mother to the greenhouse.  We had a long list of things to buy.

I was hoping to start a new herb garden.  I picked up parsley, sage, rosemary, thyme, and peppermint.  I considered garlic, lemongrass, and a few other things, but ultimately decided to start small.

Mom was focused on her vegetable garden.  She bought carrots, cucumbers, zucchini, pumpkins, peppers, tomatoes, onions, and peas.

I was impressed by the selection of trees at the greenhouse.  They had oak, maple, apple, pear, beech, and several types of pine.  There was even a tulip tree.

The checkout area smelled of jasmine.
